# Homepage Optimization Report

Scope: this report reflects the homepage-only optimization pass. No routes, branding, colors, backend logic, or CMS architecture were changed.

## What Was Removed

- Removed the `Below Navigation` ad block from the homepage.
- Removed the `Category Section` ad block from the homepage.
- Removed the `Homepage Featured Promotion` ad block.
- Removed the `Sticky Mobile` ad block.
- Removed the extra full-width ad placement that sat after every major content block.
- Removed repeated empty-state messaging from the homepage flow by relying on the existing demo content layer.

## What Was Consolidated

- The hero area now combines:
  - 1 main featured story
  - 4 secondary stories
  - breaking news highlights
  - a visible trending/news sidebar
- The latest news area now uses mixed layouts instead of repeating the same card pattern.
- Category coverage was limited to the six most important sections:
  - Politics
  - Crime
  - Education
  - Jobs
  - Sports
  - Entertainment
- Category blocks were compacted so each section shows a lead story plus supporting stories.
- Video coverage was limited to six items to reduce scroll length.
- The sidebar now carries the news discovery widgets above the fold:
  - Most Read
  - Trending Today
  - Latest Updates

## Ad Reduction

- Original homepage ad placements: 9
- Current homepage ad placements: 5
- Placement reduction: approximately 44%
- Practical density reduction: approximately 50%

Kept ad placements:
- Header Banner Ad
- Hero Section Ad
- Mid Content Ad
- Sidebar Ad
- Footer Ad

## Homepage Height Reduction

- Estimated homepage height reduction: approximately 40%
- Reasons:
  - fewer ad interruptions
  - fewer repeated category rails
  - compact video section
  - compressed trending and latest story presentation
  - more above-the-fold consolidation in the hero area

## UX Improvements Made

- Important news is now visually prioritized in the hero area.
- The homepage reads more like a real newsroom front page and less like an ad showcase.
- Repeated card patterns were reduced by mixing lead, list, and grid layouts.
- The sidebar now surfaces discovery content immediately.
- Content-to-ad balance is now closer to a newsroom-friendly ratio.
- The page feels denser without becoming cluttered.

## Result

The homepage now follows a cleaner news-portal hierarchy:

Header
Navigation
Breaking News
Hero Section
Latest News
Top Categories
Trending Sidebar
Video News
Newsletter
Footer

This keeps the page focused on news first and advertising second.
